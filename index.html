ELEMENTAL ANALYSIS OF LUNAR SURFACE

My task was to analyse the data collected from Gamma Ray Spectrometer in Lunar Prospector mission launched by NASA to analyse the radioactive elements on moon. I had to plot the elemental abundance and derived maps of radioactive elements on lunar surface.
